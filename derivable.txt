Bottom line

This log file gives you the authentication journey very clearly — who logged in, how,
whether they succeeded, how many failed attempts, and when they logged out.

What it cannot give you is the post-login product journey (what they browsed, what they
bought, what they viewed) because that lives in other services' logs.

To get the full picture you'd need to correlate this with logs from the portal/policy/document
services using the customerId or sessionId as the joining key.
